Middle Eastern Dishes

🇸🇦 Saudi Arabia
🥘 Kabsa

🧂 Ingredients:

  • Basmati rice
  • Chicken or lamb
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Kabsa spices

👨‍🍳 Preparation: Sauté onions and garlic, add meat and tomatoes, cook with spices, then add rice and simmer.

🍲 Jareesh

🧂 Ingredients:

  • Crushed wheat
  • Yogurt
  • Onion
  • Chicken
  • Spices

👨‍🍳 Preparation: Cook wheat in broth, add yogurt, stir with chicken until thick and creamy.

🇱🇧 Lebanon
🥗 Tabbouleh

🧂 Ingredients:

  • Parsley
  • Mint
  • Tomatoes
  • Bulgur
  • Lemon juice
  • Olive oil

👨‍🍳 Preparation: Finely chop all ingredients and mix with lemon and olive oil dressing.

🥣 Hummus

🧂 Ingredients:

  • Chickpeas
  • Tahini
  • Garlic
  • Lemon juice
  • Olive oil

👨‍🍳 Preparation: Blend chickpeas with tahini, lemon and garlic, top with olive oil.

🇵🇸 Palestine
🍗 Musakhan

🧂 Ingredients:

  • Chicken
  • Onions
  • Sumac
  • Taboon bread
  • Olive oil

👨‍🍳 Preparation: Caramelize onions, cook chicken with sumac and oil, layer on flatbread and bake.

🌾 Mujaddara

🧂 Ingredients:

  • Lentils
  • Rice
  • Onions
  • Olive oil

👨‍🍳 Preparation: Boil lentils and rice, top with caramelized onions fried in olive oil.

🇮🇶 Iraq
🫒 Dolma

🧂 Ingredients:

  • Grape leaves
  • Rice
  • Ground meat
  • Onions
  • Herbs
  • Tomato paste

👨‍🍳 Preparation: Mix rice with meat, onion, and herbs, wrap in grape leaves, cook in tomato broth.

🧆 Kubba

🧂 Ingredients:

  • Bulgur
  • Minced meat
  • Onions
  • Spices

👨‍🍳 Preparation: Form shell with bulgur paste, stuff with seasoned meat, shape and fry.
