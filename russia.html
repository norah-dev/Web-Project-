Beef Stroganoff

Russian Dish 🇷🇺

🧂 Ingredients:
  • Beef strips
  • Mushrooms
  • Onions
  • Sour cream
  • Butter
👨‍🍳 Preparation:

Sauté beef with onions and mushrooms, simmer in sour cream sauce and serve with rice or pasta.
