European Dishes

🇫🇷 France
🥐 Croissant

🧂 Ingredients:

  • Flour
  • Butter
  • Yeast
  • Milk
  • Sugar

👨‍🍳 Preparation: Roll dough with butter into layers, shape into crescents, and bake.

🍲 Ratatouille

🧂 Ingredients:

  • Eggplant
  • Zucchini
  • Tomatoes
  • Peppers
  • Herbs

👨‍🍳 Preparation: Slice vegetables and bake or stew with olive oil and spices.

🇮🇹 Italy
🍕 Pizza Margherita

🧂 Ingredients:

  • Dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella
  • Basil

👨‍🍳 Preparation: Top dough with sauce, cheese and basil, then bake until golden.

🍝 Lasagna

🧂 Ingredients:

  • Pasta sheets
  • Minced meat
  • Tomato sauce
  • Béchamel
  • Cheese

👨‍🍳 Preparation: Layer pasta with sauces and cheese, then bake until bubbling.

🇪🇸 Spain
🥘 Paella

🧂 Ingredients:

  • Rice
  • Saffron
  • Seafood or chicken
  • Vegetables

👨‍🍳 Preparation: Cook rice with broth, saffron, and toppings in a wide pan.

🍳 Spanish Tortilla

🧂 Ingredients:

  • Potatoes
  • Eggs
  • Onions
  • Olive oil

👨‍🍳 Preparation: Fry potatoes and onions, mix with eggs, and cook in a pan until set.

🇹🇷 Turkey
🥙 Kebab

🧂 Ingredients:

  • Lamb or Beef
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Spices

👨‍🍳 Preparation: Marinate meat with spices, skewer and grill until cooked.

🍰 Baklava

🧂 Ingredients:

  • Phyllo Dough
  • Chopped Nuts
  • Butter
  • Sugar Syrup

👨‍🍳 Preparation: Layer dough with nuts and butter, bake, then soak in syrup.
