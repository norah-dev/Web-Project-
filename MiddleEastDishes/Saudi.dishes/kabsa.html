Kabsa

Saudi Arabian Dish 🇸🇦

🧂 Ingredients:
  • Rice
  • Chicken
  • Tomatoes
  • Spices (Cardamom, Cloves, Cinnamon, etc.)
  • Onions, Garlic
👨‍🍳 Preparation:

Sauté the onions and garlic, then add meat and spices. Add tomatoes and cook thoroughly. Stir in the rice, cover with water, and simmer until everything is fully cooked and fragrant.
