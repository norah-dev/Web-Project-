Jareesh

Saudi Arabian Dish 🇸🇦

🧂 Ingredients:
  • Jareesh (crushed wheat)
  • Chicken or meat
  • Yogurt
  • Onions
  • Spices (Cumin, Black lime, Salt)
👨‍🍳 Preparation:

Cook meat with onions and spices until tender. Add jareesh and yogurt, then simmer gently for hours, stirring occasionally. Serve warm and topped with sautéed onions if desired.
