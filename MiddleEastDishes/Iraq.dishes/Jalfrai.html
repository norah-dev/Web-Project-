Jalfrai

Iraqian Dish 🇮🇶

🧂 Ingredients:
  • Meat (usually beef or lamb)
  • Bell peppers
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions, Garlic
  • Spices (Cumin, Coriander, Chili, etc.)
👨‍🍳 Preparation:

Sauté onions and garlic, add meat and spices, and cook until browned. Add chopped bell peppers and tomatoes. Simmer until the vegetables are soft and sauce is thickened.
