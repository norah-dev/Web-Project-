Iraqi Dish 🇮🇶

🧂 Ingredients:
  • Grape leaves / vegetables (zucchini, eggplant, peppers)
  • Rice
  • Ground beef or lamb
  • Tomato paste
  • Spices (salt, pepper, cumin)
👨‍🍳 Preparation:

Stuff vegetables or grape leaves with rice-meat mixture. Stack tightly in pot, add tomato sauce, simmer until tender. Serve warm with yogurt.

Iraqi Dish 🇮🇶

🧂 Ingredients:
  • Beef or lamb chunks
  • Bell peppers
  • Onion
  • Tomato sauce
  • Iraqi spices (cumin, coriander, chili, black pepper)
👨‍🍳 Preparation:

Sauté onions and peppers in oil. Add meat and cook until browned. Stir in tomato sauce and spices. Simmer until meat is tender. Serve with rice or bread.
