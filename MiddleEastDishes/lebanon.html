Tabbouleh

Lebanese Dish 🇱🇧

🧂 Ingredients:
  • Parsley
  • Tomatoes
  • Mint
  • Onion
  • Bulgur (fine)
  • Lemon juice
  • Olive oil
👨‍🍳 Preparation:

Finely chop vegetables and herbs. Soak bulgur briefly. Mix all ingredients with lemon juice and olive oil. Serve chilled as a fresh salad.

Baked Kibbeh

Lebanese Dish 🇱🇧

🧂 Ingredients:
  • Fine bulgur
  • Ground beef or lamb
  • Onions
  • Spices (allspice, cinnamon)
  • Olive oil or ghee
👨‍🍳 Preparation:

Mix bulgur with spiced meat to form a dough. Spread in a tray with a meat filling in between two layers. Cut into diamonds, drizzle with oil, and bake until golden.
