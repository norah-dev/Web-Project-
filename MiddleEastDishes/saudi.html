Kabsa

Saudi Dish 🇸🇦

🧂 Ingredients:
  • Rice (long grain)
  • Chicken or lamb
  • Tomatoes
  • Spices (cardamom, cinnamon, cloves, bay leaf)
  • Onions and garlic
  • Raisins and nuts (optional)
👨‍🍳 Preparation:

Cook meat with spices and tomatoes. Add rice and simmer until fully cooked and flavorful. Garnish with nuts or raisins if desired.

Jareesh

Saudi Dish 🇸🇦

🧂 Ingredients:
  • Crushed wheat (jareesh)
  • Yogurt
  • Onions
  • Butter or ghee
  • Salt, cumin
  • Chicken or meat broth
👨‍🍳 Preparation:

Cook crushed wheat in broth until tender. Add yogurt and simmer while stirring. Finish with sautéed onions and melted ghee on top.
