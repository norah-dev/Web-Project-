Mujaddara

Palestinian Dish 🇵🇸

🧂 Ingredients:
  • Lentils
  • Rice or bulgur
  • Onions
  • Olive oil
  • Salt, cumin
👨‍🍳 Preparation:

Cook lentils until tender, then mix with rice and spices. Top with caramelized onions fried in olive oil. Serve warm or at room temperature.

Musakhan

Palestinian Dish 🇵🇸

🧂 Ingredients:
  • Chicken
  • Onions
  • Olive oil
  • Sumac
  • Taboon bread (or flatbread)
  • Pine nuts (optional)
👨‍🍳 Preparation:

Cook onions with olive oil and sumac. Roast chicken separately, then serve over bread topped with the onion mixture. Garnish with pine nuts if desired and bake briefly.
