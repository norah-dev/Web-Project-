South American Dishes

🇲🇽 Mexico
🌮 Tacos

🧂 Ingredients:

  • Tortillas
  • Beef or Chicken
  • Vegetables
  • Cheese

👨‍🍳 Preparation: Fill tortillas with meat or veggies, top with cheese, serve warm.

🌯 Enchiladas

🧂 Ingredients:

  • Tortillas
  • Shredded Chicken
  • Enchilada Sauce
  • Cheese

👨‍🍳 Preparation: Roll chicken in tortillas, pour sauce over, sprinkle cheese, and bake.

🇧🇷 Brazil
🍲 Feijoada

🧂 Ingredients:

  • Black beans
  • Pork or Beef
  • Garlic
  • Onions

👨‍🍳 Preparation: Cook beans with meat and spices until tender.

🧀 Pão de Queijo

🧂 Ingredients:

  • Tapioca flour
  • Cheese
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Butter

👨‍🍳 Preparation: Mix all ingredients, shape into balls, and bake.

🇨🇴 Colombia
🫓 Arepas

🧂 Ingredients:

  • Cornmeal
  • Cheese
  • Butter
  • Optional Meat

👨‍🍳 Preparation: Form dough from cornmeal and cheese, grill or bake.

🍛 Bandeja Paisa

🧂 Ingredients:

  • Rice
  • Beans
  • Ground Meat
  • Chicharrón
  • Fried Egg
  • Plantain
  • Avocado

👨‍🍳 Preparation: Assemble all ingredients on a large plate and serve.

🇨🇺 Cuba
🥩 Ropa Vieja

🧂 Ingredients:

  • Shredded Beef
  • Peppers
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes

👨‍🍳 Preparation: Cook beef with veggies and seasonings until tender.

🍌 Tostones

🧂 Ingredients:

  • Green Plantains
  • Oil
  • Salt

👨‍🍳 Preparation: Slice plantains, fry, smash, and fry again until crispy.
