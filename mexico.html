Enchiladas

Mexican Dish 🇲🇽

🧂 Ingredients:
  • Corn tortillas
  • Chicken or beef
  • Enchilada sauce
  • Cheese
  • Onions
👨‍🍳 Preparation:

Fill tortillas with meat, roll, cover with sauce and cheese, and bake until bubbly.
