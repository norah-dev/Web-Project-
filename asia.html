Asian Dishes

🇯🇵 Japan
🍣 Sushi

🧂 Ingredients:

  • Rice
  • Fish
  • Seaweed
  • Vinegar

👨‍🍳 Preparation: Roll rice and fish in seaweed, serve chilled.

🍜 Ramen

🧂 Ingredients:

  • Noodles
  • Broth
  • Boiled egg
  • Sliced pork
  • Green onions

👨‍🍳 Preparation: Cook noodles in broth, add toppings like egg and pork.

🇰🇷 Korea
🍚 Bibimbap

🧂 Ingredients:

  • Rice
  • Assorted vegetables
  • Egg
  • Gochujang (chili paste)
  • Beef

👨‍🍳 Preparation: Place rice in bowl, top with vegetables, beef, egg, and chili paste.

🥬 Kimchi

🧂 Ingredients:

  • Napa cabbage
  • Korean chili powder
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Fish sauce

👨‍🍳 Preparation: Ferment cabbage with spices and seasonings for several days.

🇨🇳 China
🥟 Dumplings

🧂 Ingredients:

  • Flour
  • Meat or vegetables
  • Soy sauce
  • Garlic

👨‍🍳 Preparation: Wrap filling in dough, steam or fry.

🍗 Sweet and Sour Chicken

🧂 Ingredients:

  • Chicken
  • Bell peppers
  • Pineapple
  • Vinegar
  • Sugar
  • Ketchup

👨‍🍳 Preparation: Fry chicken, stir with vegetables and sweet-sour sauce.

🇹🇭 Thailand
🍜 Pad Thai

🧂 Ingredients:

  • Rice noodles
  • Shrimp
  • Eggs
  • Peanuts
  • Bean sprouts

👨‍🍳 Preparation: Stir-fry noodles with shrimp, eggs, and sauce, top with peanuts.

🍲 Tom Yum Soup

🧂 Ingredients:

  • Shrimp
  • Lemongrass
  • Kaffir lime leaves
  • Chili
  • Mushrooms
  • Lime juice

👨‍🍳 Preparation: Boil broth with herbs and spices, add shrimp and mushrooms, finish with lime juice.

🇷🇺 Russia
🥣 Borscht

🧂 Ingredients:

  • Beets
  • Cabbage
  • Carrots
  • Onions
  • Sour cream

👨‍🍳 Preparation: Boil vegetables in broth, serve with sour cream.

🥩 Beef Stroganoff

🧂 Ingredients:

  • Beef strips
  • Mushrooms
  • Onions
  • Sour cream
  • Mustard

👨‍🍳 Preparation: Sauté beef with onions and mushrooms, stir in sour cream and mustard.
