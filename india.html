Biryani

Indian Dish 🇮🇳

🧂 Ingredients:
  • Basmati rice
  • Chicken or lamb
  • Yogurt
  • Onions
  • Spices
👨‍🍳 Preparation:

Marinate meat, layer with rice and cook with steam for deep flavor.
