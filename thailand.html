Pad Thai

Thai Dish 🇹🇭

🧂 Ingredients:
  • Rice noodles
  • Tofu or shrimp
  • Egg
  • Peanuts
  • Tamarind sauce
👨‍🍳 Preparation:

Stir-fry noodles with sauce and ingredients, garnish with peanuts.

Tom Yum Soup

Thai Dish 🇹🇭

🧂 Ingredients:
  • Shrimp
  • Lemongrass
  • Kaffir lime leaves
  • Galangal
  • Chili
  • Mushrooms
👨‍🍳 Preparation:

Boil shrimp with herbs and spices to make a spicy and sour soup.
