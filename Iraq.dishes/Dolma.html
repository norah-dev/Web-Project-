Dolma

Iraqian Dish 🇮🇶

🧂 Ingredients:
  • Grape leaves
  • Rice
  • Ground beef or lamb
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions, Garlic
  • Spices (Salt, Pepper, Allspice)
👨‍🍳 Preparation:

Mix rice, meat, chopped tomatoes, onions, garlic, and spices. Stuff grape leaves with the mixture, roll tightly, and layer in a pot. Cover with water and lemon juice, then simmer until tender.
