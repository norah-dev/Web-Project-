Hummus

Lebanese Dish 🇱🇧

🧂 Ingredients:
  • Chickpeas (boiled)
  • Tahini
  • Lemon juice
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil, Salt
👨‍🍳 Preparation:

Blend chickpeas, tahini, lemon juice, and garlic until smooth. Add olive oil and salt to taste. Serve with a drizzle of olive oil on top.
