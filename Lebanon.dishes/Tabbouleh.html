tabbouleh

Lebanese Dish 🇱🇧

🧂 Ingredients:
  • Parsley (finely chopped)
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Bulgur
  • Lemon juice
  • Olive oil, Salt
👨‍🍳 Preparation:

Soak bulgur in water until soft. Finely chop parsley, tomatoes, and onions. Mix everything together and season with lemon juice, olive oil, and salt.
