Maskhan

palesetinan Dish 🇵🇸

🧂 Ingredients:
  • Taboon bread or flatbread
  • Chicken
  • Onions (lots of them!)
  • Olive oil
  • Sumac, Salt, Pepper, Spices
  • Pine nuts (optional)
👨‍🍳 Preparation:

Sauté sliced onions in plenty of olive oil until soft and golden. Add sumac and spices, then mix with cooked, shredded chicken. Spread the mixture over bread and bake until the edges are crispy. Garnish with toasted pine nuts if desired.
