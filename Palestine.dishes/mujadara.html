palesetinan Dish 🇵🇸

🧂 Ingredients:
  • Brown or green lentils
  • Rice or bulgur
  • Onions
  • Olive oil
  • Salt, Cumin
👨‍🍳 Preparation:

Boil lentils until halfway cooked. Add rice or bulgur and cook together until soft. Meanwhile, fry onions in olive oil until dark brown and crispy. Mix some onions into the lentils and rice, and use the rest as garnish on top.
