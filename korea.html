Kimchi

Korean Dish 🇰🇷

🧂 Ingredients:
  • Cabbage
  • Chili powder
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Salted fish
👨‍🍳 Preparation:

Ferment spiced cabbage in a jar for a few days to develop flavor.

Bibimbap

Korean Dish 🇰🇷

🧂 Ingredients:
  • Rice
  • Vegetables (carrot, spinach, mushrooms, etc.)
  • Egg
  • Gochujang (Korean chili paste)
  • Beef (optional)
👨‍🍳 Preparation:

Arrange cooked vegetables and beef over rice, top with a fried egg and gochujang sauce.
