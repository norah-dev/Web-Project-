Feijoada

Brazilian Dish 🇧🇷

🧂 Ingredients:
  • Black beans
  • Pork
  • Sausage
  • Garlic
  • Onions
👨‍🍳 Preparation:

Cook beans with meats and seasonings slowly until thick and flavorful.

Pão de Queijo

Brazilian Snack 🇧🇷

🧂 Ingredients:
  • Tapioca flour
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Oil
  • Grated cheese (Parmesan or mozzarella)
👨‍🍳 Preparation:

Mix ingredients to form a dough, shape into small balls, and bake until golden and puffed.
