Lasagna

Italian Dish 🇮🇹

🧂 Ingredients:
  • Lasagna pasta sheets
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Onion, garlic, herbs
👨‍🍳 Preparation:

Cook ground beef with onions and tomato sauce. Layer pasta sheets with meat, ricotta, and cheese. Repeat layers, top with mozzarella and parmesan. Bake until golden and bubbly.

Pizza

Italian Dish 🇮🇹

🧂 Ingredients:
  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil
  • Olive oil
  • Optional toppings (pepperoni, veggies, etc.)
👨‍🍳 Preparation:

Spread tomato sauce over rolled dough. Add mozzarella and desired toppings. Bake in a hot oven until crust is crisp and cheese is melted. Top with fresh basil and a drizzle of olive oil.
