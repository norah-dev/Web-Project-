Menemen

Turkish Dish 🇹🇷

🧂 Ingredients:
  • Eggs
  • Tomatoes
  • Green peppers
  • Onions (optional)
  • Olive oil
  • Salt, pepper, paprika
👨‍🍳 Preparation:

Sauté chopped onions and green peppers in olive oil. Add diced tomatoes and cook until softened. Crack the eggs over the mixture and stir gently. Season and cook until the eggs are just set. Serve hot with bread.
