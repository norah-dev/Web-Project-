Mantı

Turkish Dish 🇹🇷

🧂 Ingredients:
  • Flour, water, eggs (for dough)
  • Ground beef or lamb
  • Onion
  • Salt, pepper
  • Yogurt
  • Garlic
  • Butter, paprika, mint
👨‍🍳 Preparation:

Prepare dough and roll it thin. Mix meat with onion, salt, and pepper. Cut dough into small squares, fill each with meat, and seal them. Boil until tender. Serve with garlic yogurt and a hot butter-paprika sauce on top.
