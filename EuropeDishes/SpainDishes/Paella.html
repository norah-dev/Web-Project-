Paella

Spanish Dish 🇪🇸

🧂 Ingredients:
  • Rice (short grain)
  • Shrimp, mussels, chicken
  • Bell peppers, peas
  • Onions, garlic
  • Tomatoes
  • Olive oil
  • Saffron, paprika, salt, pepper
👨‍🍳 Preparation:

Sauté onions, garlic, and bell peppers in olive oil. Add chicken and cook until browned. Stir in rice and spices. Add tomatoes, broth, seafood, and vegetables. Simmer uncovered until rice is tender and liquid is absorbed.
