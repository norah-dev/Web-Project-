Tortilla Española

Spanish Dish 🇪🇸

🧂 Ingredients:
  • Potatoes
  • Eggs
  • Onions (optional)
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
👨‍🍳 Preparation:

Thinly slice and fry potatoes (and onions if using) in olive oil until soft. Beat the eggs with salt, then combine with drained potatoes. Pour the mixture into a skillet and cook slowly on both sides until firm and golden. Serve warm or at room temperature.
