Margherita Pizza

Italian Dish 🇮🇹

🧂 Ingredients:
  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Fresh mozzarella
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
👨‍🍳 Preparation:

Spread tomato sauce over rolled-out pizza dough. Add sliced mozzarella and fresh basil leaves. Drizzle with olive oil and a pinch of salt. Bake in a hot oven until crust is golden and cheese is bubbling.
