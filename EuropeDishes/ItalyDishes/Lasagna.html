Lasagna

Italian Dish 🇮🇹

🧂 Ingredients:
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Onion, garlic
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella & Parmesan
  • Salt, pepper, Italian herbs
👨‍🍳 Preparation:

Cook beef with onion, garlic, and tomato sauce. Layer cooked noodles with ricotta, meat sauce, and cheeses in a baking dish. Repeat layers and top with mozzarella and Parmesan. Bake until bubbly and golden on top.
