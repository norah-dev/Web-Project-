Paella

Spanish Dish 🇪🇸

🧂 Ingredients:
  • Rice
  • Saffron
  • Chicken or seafood
  • Bell peppers
  • Green peas
  • Garlic, onion, olive oil
👨‍🍳 Preparation:

Sauté chicken or seafood with vegetables. Add rice and saffron-infused broth. Cook uncovered until rice is tender and liquid absorbed. Garnish and serve hot.

Tortilla Española

Spanish Dish 🇪🇸

🧂 Ingredients:
  • Potatoes
  • Eggs
  • Onions
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
👨‍🍳 Preparation:

Thinly slice and fry potatoes with onions. Beat eggs and mix with cooked potatoes. Pour into a pan and cook until firm on both sides. Slice and serve warm or cold.
