Croissant

French Dish 🇫🇷

🧂 Ingredients:
  • Flour
  • Butter (lots of it!)
  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Yeast
  • Salt
  • Egg (for egg wash)
👨‍🍳 Preparation:

Make a yeast dough and let it rise. Roll and fold the dough with butter multiple times to create layers (lamination). Shape into crescents and let them proof. Brush with egg wash and bake until golden and flaky.
