Croissant

French Dish 🇫🇷

🧂 Ingredients:
  • Flour
  • Butter (lots of it!)
  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Yeast
  • Salt
  • Egg (for egg wash)
👨‍🍳 Preparation:

Make a yeast dough and let it rise. Roll and fold the dough with butter multiple times to create layers (lamination). Shape into crescents and let them proof. Brush with egg wash and bake until golden and flaky.

Ratatouille

French Dish 🇫🇷

🧂 Ingredients:
  • Eggplant
  • Zucchini
  • Tomatoes
  • Bell peppers
  • Onions, garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Salt, pepper, thyme, basil
👨‍🍳 Preparation:

Slice all vegetables evenly. Sauté onions and garlic in olive oil, then layer vegetables in a baking dish in a circular pattern. Season with herbs, cover with foil, and bake until tender. Optionally drizzle with more olive oil before serving.
